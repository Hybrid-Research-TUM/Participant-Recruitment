A Study on
Space & Reluctance

We are studying the reality of hybrid work—the good, the bad, and the glitchy.

Driven by recent data indicating that nearly 60% of remote-capable workers now prefer hybrid or fully remote arrangements, this research investigates the specific 'spatial obstacles' of the modern workplace. We aim to understand exactly why the home office is increasingly preferred, analyzing which attributes of corporate innovation spaces are now rejected as dysfunctional or unappealing.
