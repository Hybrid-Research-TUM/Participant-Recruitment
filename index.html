A Study on Space & Reluctance

We are studying the reality of hybrid work—the good, the bad, and the glitchy. We specifically want to understand why many professionals prefer home offices over corporate spaces.

Motivated by data indicating that 60% of remote-capable workers specifically prefer a hybrid arrangement, this research examines which aspects of the office are rejected as dysfunctional or unappealing.
